Welcome

Welcome to our experiment! In this experiment, we would like you to imagine yourself in the role of a successful and busy mortgage broker. Mortgage brokers are professionals who help clients secure home loans from banks or other lending institutions. You will be presented with 25 profiles of people who are looking to buy real estate and have decided to find a broker to help them secure a mortgage. You will learn a little bit about each of them, including their income, education level, total assets, self employment status, and number of dependents. Your task is to decide as quickly as possible whether you will take them on as a client, because competition between mortgage brokers for good clients is fierce.

It is important that you make an accurate decision. So in this experiment we will be providing you with some state-of-the-art help, AI. After you get familiar with how to spot a good client on your own with 10 potential clients, we will offer you a selection of two different AI assistants that will give you a recommendation for each task. Note that the AI is not always correct. You will need to decide which AI you trust the most to help you maximize your earnings.

Mortgage brokers are paid a commission which is a percentage of the total value of the mortgage, 1% in our case. The more successful mortgages a broker secures, the more money they make. However, if you take on clients that don't get approved for a mortgage, you have wasted your time. As well, if pass over clients who eventually get approved, you have also lost out on potential earnings. In both of these cases you will lose 0.5% of the total value of the mortgage. Finally, if you don't take on a client, and the bank agrees they are not a good candidate for a mortgage, you will not lose or gain any money.

Here is an example of how it works:
  • If you take on a client who is approved for a $500,000 mortgage, you will earn $5,000.
  • If you pass on a client who is approved for a $500,000 mortgage, you will lose $2,500.
  • If you take on a client who is not approved for a $500,000 mortgage, you will lose $2,500.
  • If you pass on a client who is not approved for a mortgage, you will not lose or gain any money.


    • At the end of the game you will see how much you have been able to earn. Your score will go up on a "Leadboard" and you will be able to see how you compare to other players. Here is the order of things you will be asked to do:

  • In the first 10 tasks, you are required to make your own decisions.
  • After 10 tasks, you will have an additional 8 tasks to work separately with each AI assistant (4 + 4)
  • In the last 7 tasks, you will choose which assistant to work with on each task. Note that the AIs are not always correct. You can decide which one to trust.
  • Once you have completed all the tasks there will be a short survey, after which we will show how you did against all other participants in the study.


    • The whole process will take you about 20 minutes. And we will pay a 1 USD bonus if your decision accuracy in the last 20 tasks rank top 10% in all survey participants.
